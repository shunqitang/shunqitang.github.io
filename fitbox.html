Fitbox

OVERVIEW

The goal of this individual project is to investigate the obstacles people encounter when exercising at home and propose effective solution to overcome them and achieving positive results. As a final result, FitBox is designed for students to improve their home workout experience during the lockdown.

MY ROLE

User research, Data analysis, Prototyping, UI design, Product design, 3D modeling, Usability test

September 2020 - January 2021 (40 working days)

Context

Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, daily activities have changed significantly. During the lockdown period, I noticed that my sportive friends lacked motivation to stay fit at home. So I decided to explore the shift towards home workouts in this project.

User Journey Map

To gain a comprehensive understanding of this problem, I conducted interviews with six sportive students and observed the home workout routines of two participants. 67% of the participants start their home workout with a negative emotion (reluctant and pushed).
